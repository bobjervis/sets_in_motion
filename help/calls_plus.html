Callerlab Plus Level Calls

These calls are organized by the Callerlab call names in the table of contents of the Callerlab Plus Definitions document dated December 10, 2007.

Sections are included below for each call, even if there is nothing said in that section.  If nothing is said, then the call should work as you would expect.

1. Acey Deucey

2. All 8 Spin the Top

3. (Anything) and Roll

The call "roll" is defined as a standa-lone call, so it can be written on a line by itself, or added as an "and roll" suffix to most any other call.

4. (Anything) and Spread

The call is not yet fully implemented.  It will work for many preceding calls, but not all.  This is a known bug.

5. Chase Right

6. Coordinate

This call is restricted to columns at the Plus level.  Other starting formations are used, particularly at higher levels, but they stretch the definition a bit and are always considered to be hard variations.

7. Crossfire

8. Cut the Diamond

9. Diamond Circulate

10. Dixie Grand

11. Explode and (Anything)

The call "Explode" can be written by itself and means "Explode and do nothing".

12. Extend

13. Fan the Top

14. Flip the Diamond

15. Follow your Neighbor

16. Grand Swing Thru

17. Linear Cycle

18. Load the Boat

The definitions work in a variety of starting formations besides facing lines.  Definitions are also included for "do the centers/ends part of load the boat" as four-dancer calls.

19. Peel off

20. Peel the Top

21. Ping Pong Circulate

22. Relay the Deucey

23. Single Circle to a Wave

24. Spin Chain and Exchange the Gears

A variant definition is included to "turn the stars" by a given fraction.

25. Spin Chain the Gears

A variant definition is include to "turn the stars" by a given fraction.

26. Teacup Chain 

27. 3/4 Tag the Line

The definition allows for any fraction, not just 3/4.

28. Track 2

29. Trade the Wave
