Performance Window

The separate window that you use to read a sequence, typically performing the sequence during a dance, is called the Performance Window.

At right you can see an image of a Performance Window loaded with a Basic level sequence.  As you click the space bar, the reverse-video bar advances to the next call.  As the bar gets to the bottom of the screen, the window will scroll to keep the highlighted bar in view at all times.

When you are done with a sequence, you can press the Enter key to advance to the next sequence (if you are performing more than one sequence at a time).  The following is a complete list of the keystrokes and what they do in the Performance Window:

  • Enter - Advances to the next sequence being performed.
  • + - Same as Enter.
  • - - Backs up one sequence.
  • Space Bar - Advances one call.
  • Up Arrow - Backs up one sequence.
  • Down Arrow - Advances one sequence.
  • Left Arrow - Backs up one sequence.
  • Right Arrow - Advances one sequence.

In the future, this window will be expanded in both capability and flexibility.
